## What you can do

You may:
- use this software internally
- modify it
- deploy it on your own servers
- run it as part of your accounting/compliance operations

## What happens if you modify and deploy it

If you modify this software and deploy it (including as a hosted service),
you must make your modified source code available to the users of that service,
as required by AGPLv3.

## What you do NOT have to publish

You are not required to publish:
- your accounting data
- your cost rates
- your customer or contract information
- your internal policies
- any unrelated internal software

Only modifications to this software itself must be shared.

## Why AGPL

GovCon compliance software is frequently sold in closed and expensive ecosystems.
AGPL ensures this project cannot be captured into a proprietary product without
contributing improvements back to the community.
